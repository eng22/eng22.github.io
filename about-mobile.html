About
Micro-Cosmos

A sci-fi audio drama about a crew of terraformers on a planet many, many stars away. Produced by Futuristic Trail Mix Productions. Written by Zyrel Thompson, Jesse Smith, and Lauren Tucker.

The adventure begins March 2021
